Mind Games

bzû is an immersive adventure game that takes players on a mesmerizing journey through the depths of the ocean. Developed by Giant Squid Studios and released in 2016, Abzû invites players to explore vibrant underwater environments teeming with marine life. Through its stunning visuals, evocative soundtrack, and meditative gameplay, Abzû offers a serene and therapeutic experience, allowing players to unwind and immerse themselves in the beauty and tranquility of the ocean depths.

Stardew Valley is a popular farming simulation game that invites players into a peaceful world where they can cultivate crops, raise animals, and build relationships with charming villagers. Beyond its engaging gameplay, many find it to be a soothing escape that promotes relaxation and reduces stress through its serene atmosphere and rewarding activities.

Elegy for a Dead World: This game allows you to explore different worlds and write stories about them. ThIS game encourages introspection and creativity as players contemplate the histories and mysteries of these worlds, making it a thought-provoking experience. Through its interactive storytelling and evocative visuals, "Elegy for a Dead World" serves as both a creative outlet and a reflection on themes of loss, memory, and imagination.

Journey is a visually stunning adventure game where players control a robed traveler in a vast desert, journeying towards a distant mountain. The game emphasizes exploration, atmospheric storytelling, and emotional connection without using any spoken or written language. Players can encounter other travelers (played by real people online) along the way, fostering a sense of companionship and shared experience.
